Once the planetesimals have formed, they can now enter the next phase where they accrete matter (dust and gas) to become protoplanets. Now this process can occur through two possible mechanisms:
- [[knowledge/Physics and Chemistry of Planet Formation/Planetesimal Accretion|Planetesimal Accretion]]
- [[knowledge/Physics and Chemistry of Planet Formation/Pebble Accretion|Pebble Accretion]]

>This is a live document comprising of information on the instruments aboard the CSST. 

For our purposes, our focus will be on the *Cool Planet Imaging Coronagraph* or CPI-C, on of the five back-end instruments aboard the CSST. It is designed for ultra-high contrast detection and multi-band photometry studied of extrasolar planets and circumstellar disks in visible to infrared wavelengths.
